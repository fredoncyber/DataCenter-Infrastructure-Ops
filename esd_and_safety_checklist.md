# ESD & Safety Checklist (Field)

- [ ] Wear ESD wrist strap; verify ground point.
- [ ] Use ESD-safe surface/mat for components.
- [ ] Power down equipment and confirm both power feeds are disconnected before service.
- [ ] Observe hot/cold aisle containment and airflow direction.
- [ ] Lift with legs, not back; use two-person lift for >35 lbs.
- [ ] Keep fiber/copper runs unstrained; observe minimum bend radius.
- [ ] Keep tools organized; no loose screws in chassis.
- [ ] Verify no labels/loose velcro obstructs fans.
