# Mapping to Google Data Center Technician Responsibilities

- **Deploy and operate new data center infrastructure**  
  Labs **01** and **03**: rack assembly checklist, cabling, and baseline network capture.

- **Troubleshoot and diagnose issues with equipment/infra**  
  Lab **02**: `server_health_check.sh` (CPU/Mem/Disk/SMART) and `linux_log_analysis.sh` (journal/syslog).

- **Maintain security & integrity of data; media tracking**  
  Lab **04**: sanitization SOP, media tracking CSV, asset decommissioning form.

- **Disassemble equipment at end‑of‑life (EoL)**  
  Lab **04**: decommissioning form + checklist for part replacement and chain of custody.

- **Repair/fix & preventive maintenance**  
  Lab **02** + `SOPs/preventive_maintenance_schedule.md`.
